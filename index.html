Xiangyu Xu

I am a research scientist at Sea AI Lab, where I work on computer vision and machine learning.

I've worked on RawSR, QVI, RSC-Net, and Texformer. I got my BEng and BEc degrees from THU and PKU. I did my PhD at THU and my postdoc at CMU and MIT. I was a research fellow at NTU and a research scientist at SenseTime.
